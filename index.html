Hello there! I am an year old Zimbabwe - South Sudanese student passionate about using technology to make the world a better place.


I'm deeply interested in robotics, astronomy and environmental sustainability. Over the years, i've tried to build up some solid leadership and engineering skills, some of which are evident in my projects and activities below.


I love building stuff and in my spare time you'll probably find me coding, planning out my next big project or playing chess. I've also recently dived into the world of ethical hacking and cybersecurity.


Feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate on a project or just say hi!
